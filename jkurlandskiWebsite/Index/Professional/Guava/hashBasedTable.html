More than once I've needed to work with a database table-like structure in memory--a container offering reasonably fast lookups on data having a "row and column" type of structure. Usually what I really want to do is optionally (and efficiently) sort on any column. For example, suppose we have an in-memory Movie table like the one at right.

I'd like one of my objects to hold this structure as a component and offer its users the ability to retrieve the data sorted on any column.
