We may characterize the union and intersection operations in terms of multisubsets.

Definition. A multiset f:A→Knormal-:fnormal-→AKf:A\to K is a multisubset of a multiset g:B→Knormal-:gnormal-→BKg:B\to K if

We write f⊆gfgf\subseteq g to mean that fff is a multisubset of ggg.

Proposition 1.

Given multisets fff and ggg.

  • f∪gfgf\cup g is the smallest multiset such that fff and ggg are multisubsets of it. In other words, if f⊆hfhf\subseteq h and g⊆hghg\subseteq h, then f∪g⊆hfghf\cup g\subseteq h.

  • f∩gfgf\cap g is the largest multiset that is a multisubset of fff and ggg. In other words, if h⊆fhfh\subseteq f and h⊆ghgh\subseteq g, then h⊆f∩ghfgh\subseteq f\cap g.

Remark. One may also define the powerset of a multiset fff: the multiset such that each of its elements is a multisubset of fff. However, the resulting multiset is just a set (the multiplicity of each element is 111).
