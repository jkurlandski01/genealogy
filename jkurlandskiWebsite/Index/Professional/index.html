Welcome to Close to the Machine, where I post a few things learned in my professional and private experience in software development.

I borrow the title of this site from Ellen Ullman's Close to the Machine: Technophilia and Its Discontents. The back cover of my copy calls the book "a candid account of the life of a software engineer." The book certainly is candid, no doubt about it. Although I don't intend to emulate Ullman in the candor department, I do like the title of her book enough to appropriate it for my website.

In these pages I hope eventually to cover topics such as the following:

  • Tips on some of Google's Guava libraries
  • Using the ConceptNet semantic network
  • Working with the Rotten Tomatoes API
  • Intermediate CSS and HTML
