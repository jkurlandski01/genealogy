Greetings

Welcome to Adventures in Genealogy, which tracks my discoveries and missteps as I try to shed some light on my ancestors.

Here I cover branches of the following St. Louis-based clans:

  • The Kurlandskis
  • The AuBuchons
  • The Nawrockis
  • Other families tangentially related to the above

Start by clicking on one of the tabs at the top.

Keep in mind that this is a work in progress. I make no claim that this material is complete or error-free. In fact, I'm eager to learn of any corrections or additional information you might have.
