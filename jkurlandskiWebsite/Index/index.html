Greetings

Welcome to Adventures in Genealogy, which tracks my discoveries and missteps as I try to shed some light on my ancestors.

Here I cover branches of the following St. Louis-based clans:

  • The Kurlandskis
  • The AuBuchons
  • The Nawrockis
  • Other families tangentially related to the above

Start by clicking on one of the tabs at the top.

Keep in mind that this is a work in progress. I make no claim that this material is complete or error-free. In fact, I'm eager to learn of any corrections or additional information you might have.

Under the Hood

Like the contents, the design of this website is an ongoing process which, hopefully, will give me a chance to learn a little more about web development. Details below.

Formatting

The formatting of this page--indeed, this website--depends on a cascading style sheet (a .css file) which I obtained from FreeCssTemplates.org.

As per their Creative Commons License, you'll find a link to their wonderful tools below.

I'm too proud not to point out, however, that the changes I have made to the FreeCss template I borrowed have been so extensive that you may be hard put to identify it as you peruse the site.

As is evident from the formatting here and in other sections of this website, I have yet to master my cascading style sheet. But I am working on it...
