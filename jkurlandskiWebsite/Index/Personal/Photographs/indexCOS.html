COS Conference, May 1986


A year after the All-Vol Conference, my group returned to the Samir Hotel in Mohammedia for our Close-of-Service Conference. I remember resume-writing, interview practice and a conga line whose tail-end climbed back through the window just as the song stopped—a grande finale extraordinaire.
