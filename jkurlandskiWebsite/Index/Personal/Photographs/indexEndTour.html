End-of-Service Tours

After the Close-of-Service Conference, I paid visit to some towns in Morocco I hadn't seen before, including Beni Mellal. Then a month or two later I took a grand tour around the country. I started from my home in Sidi Bennour, passing through Casablanca to get to Rabat. Then I headed for Fez, making stops in Azrou and Midelt on the way to Er Rachidia on the other side of the Atlas Mountains. There began a leisurely tour along the road to Ouarzazate--the western edge of the Sahara desert. After that, back over the mountains to Marrakesh, and back home to Sidi Bennour.
