Nawrocki Family History

Here is a first-step in the effort of documenting my father's mother's side of the family--the Nawrockis. The links at right will lead you to what I have so far managed to learn and write up.

Some other surnames mentioned in these pages:

  • Kania
  • Jurkiewicz
  • Januchowski
  • Jankowski
  • Maslanka
  • Lewandowski

As you can see, some of these names are a real mouthful, and--as you might imagine--hard to keep straight. If I've made any mistakes, please feel free to let me know.
