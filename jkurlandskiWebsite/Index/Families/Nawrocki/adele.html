Adele Nawrocki,

alias Mimi

This page concerns the early years of my paternal grandmother, Adele Kurlandski, nee Nawrocki. Her first grandchild, Cathy Kurlandski (later Cathy Schwartz) began calling her “Mimi” when she was just learning to talk, and the nickname stuck.

Casmir and Cecilia

Wedding Invitation, front

My father is Gerald (Jerry) Kurlandski; his mother was Adele Kurlandski, nee Adele Nawrocki. Her mother and father were Casmir and Cecelia. Judging by their wedding invitations, the Polish versions of their names were Kazimir and Cecylia.
