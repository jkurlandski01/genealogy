Miscellaneous Kurlandski Photographs

Here are some miscellaneous photographs of this side of the family. Any help in identifying the people would be appreciated.

Can you guess which one of these characters is Sister Regina? Better, do you know any of the others? I believe the woman seated on the other side of the table is Sophia Kurlandski. The window I think indicates they're in a basement flat. Where?

Some Kurlandski clan members in the kitchen


My notes say this is Sophia and Louis, which surely would be her son, Louis U., rather than her husband, Louis Roy. But something about the man--the shape of his face or the tilt of his head--is strongly suggestive of Sophia's other son Lawrence, aka Father Larry.

Sophia Kurlandski and one of her sons


Here is Sophia with daughter Regina Lawrence, aka Sister Regina, whose legal name it seems was Sophia Wroblenski.

Sophia Kurlandski and Sister Regina


The photo below is of Louis U. and Adele Kurlandski--Sophia's son and daughter-in-law, and my grandparents.

Louis U. and Adele Kurlandski


Notice how we're moving through the generations: These three are my grandfather Louis U., my father Gerald R., and my uncle Louis V.

Louis U. Kurlandski and his two sons, Louis V. and Gerald R.
