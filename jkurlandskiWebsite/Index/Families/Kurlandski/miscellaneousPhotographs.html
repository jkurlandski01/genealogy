Miscellaneous Kurlandski Photographs

Here are some miscellaneous photographs of this side of the family. Any help in identifying the people and places would be appreciated.

Aunt Julie, Sister Regina, Sophia, and Aunt Margie

Here my dad, Sophia's grandson, sets the scene for the photo above:

From left to right are Aunt Julie Marchlewski (Vic's wife, you guys use to call him the Big Nickel Man because he would give you quarters from time to time), Sister Regina, Sophia, and Aunt Margie (Len Kurlandski's wife). The table looks like we just had a meal for several people, as indicated by the "card table" extension.

We lived in this basement flat at 3114 Mt. Pleasant St., St Louis, Mo., just a few short blocks from St. Hedwig's Church. This was grandma's basement—she lived on the first floor and Uncle Frank and Aunt Georgia lived on the second floor.


Sophia Kurlandski and her son, Frank

To me, this photo reveals what must have been a physical similarity between some of Sophia's sons. Something about Uncle Frank—the shape of his face or the tilt of his head—is strongly suggestive of Sophia's other son, Lawrence, aka Father Larry.


Sophia Kurlandski and Sister Regina

Here we have Sophia with daughter Regina Lawrence, aka Sister Regina, whose legal name it seems was Sophia Wroblenski.


Louis U. and Adele Kurlandski

The photo above is of Louis U. and Adele Kurlandski--Sophia's son and daughter-in-law, and my grandparents.


Louis U. Kurlandski and his two sons, Louis V. and Gerald R.

Notice how we've moved through the generations: These three above are my grandfather Louis U., my father Gerald R., and my uncle Louis V.
